  • Bundeena Drive to Marley walk

This rewarding walk from Bundeena Drive to Little Marley Beach leads through heath, past fresh water pools and creeks, and on to scenic beach views in Royal National Park.

  • Loftus loop trail

Grab your mountain bike and hit the Loftus loop trail in Sydney's Royal National Park.This 10km mountain bike ride is sure to get your heart pumping.

  • Hilltop Cottage

Hilltop Cottage offers heritage accommodation in Sydney's majestic Royal National Park.It's an ideal base to explore the surrounding walking tracks, beaches,and wildlife spotting opportunities in the park.

  • Pool Flat picnic area

Idyllic Pool Flat picnic area offers swimming and paddling on Hacking River in Royal National Park, in the popular family friendly Audley region, south of Sydney.
