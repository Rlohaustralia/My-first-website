National Parks Contact Centre

If unable to book online please call us to: buy an annual pass, apply for a concession pass, book accommodation, campgrounds, tours, or events.

  1. Call within Australia:1300 072 757 (13000 PARKS)
  2. Call from overseas: +61 2 9995 6500
  3. Contact hours: 7am to 7pm, daily. Closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day.
